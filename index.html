The Founder

PROFESSOR
RUSSELL JENSEN

Russell Jensen is not a typical gym owner. He holds a PhD in Physical Chemistry from MIT and spent 20 years teaching science at universities around the world before channeling that same methodical, curriculum-driven approach into Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

The result is a gym where nothing is left to chance. Every class has a structure. Every technique has a progression. Every student - whether they're 5 years old or 55, a complete beginner or a seasoned competitor - gets instruction designed to actually stick.

Ground Rules Academy is the highest-rated BJJ school in the Tri-Valley not because of marketing, but because of what happens on the mat every single day.

"Jiu-jitsu is the most effective vehicle I've found for building grit, humility, and self-discipline in people of all ages. Our job is to make sure the teaching is good enough that anyone can access it." - Russell Jensen, Founder & Head Professor

JIU-JITSU FOR THOSE WHO SERVED.

The We Defy Foundation uses Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu as a vehicle for healing, community, and purpose for post-9/11 combat-wounded veterans. Ground Rules Academy is proud to be one of their official training facilities in the Tri-Valley.

Veterans who qualify train alongside our regular members - fully integrated, fully welcomed, fully part of the GRA family. Jiu-jitsu's lessons of resilience, humility, and perseverance map directly onto the experience of those who've served.

"We believe the lessons of jiu-jitsu - learning to fall, getting back up, finding calm under pressure - are exactly what the We Defy community needs. We're honored to be part of their mission."

The GRA Difference

WHY THE TEACHING ACTUALLY WORKS

Most gyms teach jiu-jitsu the way it's always been taught - show a technique, drill it, roll. That works eventually. But Professor Russell spent 20 years studying how people actually learn before he ever opened this gym.

The GRA curriculum is built the same way a great university course is built: with learning objectives, logical progressions, deliberate practice, and real feedback. Beginners retain more. Intermediate students develop faster. And everyone leaves class knowing exactly what they worked on and why.

This isn't theory. It's the reason GRA is the highest-rated BJJ school in the Tri-Valley - and the reason students who've tried other gyms consistently say this feels different.

01
Structured Curriculum
Techniques are taught in deliberate sequences, not randomly. Each class builds on the last. Progress is visible and measurable.
02
Beginner-Friendly by Design
No experience? That's the point. The system is built to onboard new students without leaving them lost on the mat or intimidated off it.
03
Progress Over Perfection
Every student moves at their pace. The only competition that matters is with who you were last week.
04
Real Feedback, Every Class
Professor Russell watches every student. Corrections are specific, actionable, and delivered in a way that builds confidence rather than crushing it.
05
Community That Sticks
The Pleasanton BJJ community at GRA is tight. Training partners become friends. The gym becomes a second home. That's not an accident.

Class Descriptions

EVERY CLASS, EXPLAINED

Ages 5–7
BJJ Sprouts
Spark a lifelong love for jiu-jitsu with our littlest Sprouts! This class turns playtime into powerful foundations, blending fun with essential skills to boost coordination and self-assurance.
  • Core Focus: Basic movements, coordination, and skill development
  • Builds: Physical strength, confidence, and a sense of achievement
  • Activities: Basic movement drills, grappling games and supervised light sparring for safe, joyful exploration
Ages 8–13
BJJ Buds
Elevate our budding grapplers with deeper dives into jiu-jitsu essentials. This program fosters skill development, strategic thinking, and resilience through engaging, high-energy sessions.
  • Core Focus: Basic principles, techniques, movement patterns, and strategies with age-appropriate rigor
  • Builds: Coordination, strength, confidence, and grappling skills
  • Activities: Movement and technical drills, grappling games, and supervised sparring for fun, self-defense, and competition
Gi · All Levels
Fundamentals
Master the building blocks of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu with a streamlined curriculum crafted by experts. Efficient, effective training that gets you rolling from day one — suitable for teens and adults of all levels.
  • Core Focus: The effective application of grappling skills, principles, and techniques
  • Training Style: General and technical warmups, classic drilling, live/situational reps
  • Progression: Culminates in live sparring to solidify skills
Women Only
Women-Only Fundamentals
Empower yourself in a supportive environment dedicated to growth and confidence on the mats. Learn practical self-defense, refine techniques, and train alongside fellow women in a comfortable setting.
  • Core Focus: Practical BJJ techniques with emphasis on self-defense and sport jiu-jitsu application
  • For: Women of all experience levels — beginners welcome and encouraged
  • Benefits: Increased confidence, strength, stress relief, and a strong sense of community
All Levels
Advanced Class
Train like a black belt in an environment built for refinement and innovation. Here, we dissect challenges, build personalized systems, and roll with purpose — your input shapes the session.
  • Core Focus: Development and optimization of cohesive systems
  • Training Style: Intensive situational drilling and sparring
  • Community: Open Q&A — suggest topics, collaborate, and troubleshoot with fellow grapplers
Competition
Competition Class
Gear up for the spotlight with elite training that hones split-second decisions and instinctive reactions. Open to all ages ready to compete — consult an instructor to confirm your fit.
  • Core Focus: Resiliency, grit, decision-making, and natural reflexes under pressure
  • Training Style: Rigorous situational drilling of systems and sequences. Competition intensity sparring
  • For: Ambitious athletes striving for the podium and a championship mindset
Gi / No Gi
Open Mat
The ultimate free-flow space to experiment, connect, and evolve. No agenda, just mats, partners, and pure jiu-jitsu — perfect for sharpening what you've learned or discovering something new.
  • Core Focus: Self-directed practice and collaborative rolling
  • Training Style: Grab a partner and dive in — drill, spar, or flow as you like
  • Benefits: Builds adaptability, friendships, and that intuitive edge
